Susan’s Story

Susan has been rescuing animals for more than 20 years. Since she was young, seeing abandoned and mistreated dogs became a pain that turned into purpose.

In 2012, alongside her husband, she founded the Pró Animal NGO to give love, protection, and a new chance to those who never had anything.

Today, more than 40 dogs and cats depend entirely on the shelter. Many arrive marked by hunger, fear, and violence, but here they find treatment, care, and hope.

Without government support, the shelter survives thanks to Susan’s daily effort and your generosity.
